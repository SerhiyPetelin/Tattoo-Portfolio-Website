Core Values

Each tattoo master has his/her own principles in work and a unique style. Please read the information below carefully before making an appointment with me:

All my works are unique. This means I create one sketch only for one person, no repeats. The exceptions are paired tattoos for brothers, sisters, husband and wife, and so on.

I don't make tattoos according to someone else's sketch. Even if you've drawn it yourself.

I don’t make copies of other artists' tattoos and I can’t do it the same way someone does.

I reserve the right to refuse the client for my own reasons - for example, if we do not coincide in what we consider beautiful.

I don't make tattoos on some parts of the body, in particular, on the feet and intimate zones.

As a master, I determine the number of sessions that will be needed to complete a tattoo. I also assign correction sessions that you definitely need to attend.

How to Get a Tattoo?

01.

CONSULTATION

To begin with, we should get acquainted. I work in my own studio in Berlin and I will be glad to see you to discuss all the details of a future tattoo. In special cases we can talk in Skype or FaceTime. I will tell you the features of different types of tattoos and my principles so that you can finally make a decision.

02.

SKETCHING

You can choose one of the available sketches, or I can make a unique sketch for you. For this, you should tell me about your wishes. I will make a sketch, you can ask to make changes to it. I’m ready to edit the sketch until it looks perfect for you and me. Usually the process of the sketch approval takes up to 1 month.

03.

SETTING THE DATE

We choose a convenient date for both of us. Please note that the number of sessions may be different, with different time intervals. This is a particularly important if you don't live in Berlin. I try to adjust my schedule so that every client feels comfortable. The sooner you apply, the more convenient date and time you choose.

04.

TATTOO SESSIONS

Depending on the complexity of the tattoo, several sessions may be required, the duration of one session may also differ. Before you sign up for a session, make sure that you have enough time and you will not be in a hurry. After the end of the session, I give instructions for the aftercare and set the date of correction.
